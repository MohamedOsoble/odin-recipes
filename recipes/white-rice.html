White Rice

Description

A fluffy white rice side dish that pairs well with a lot of mains

A staple in many cuisines that pairs well with many main dishes as a side dish

Ingredients

  • White Rice 250g (Any variety will do)
  • Salt
  • Water

Method

  1. Pour out 250g of the rice into a bowl
  2. Rinse the rice in lukewarm water 4 times
  3. Drain the rice
  4. Place the rice into rice-cooker bowl
  5. Cover rice in water until it covers the rice by about 1.5cm
  6. Turn the rice cooker on
  7. Wait until its done. Viola, perfectly cooked fluffy rice
  8. Use a fork to fluff up the rice
