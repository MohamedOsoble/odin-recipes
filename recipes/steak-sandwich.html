Steak Sandwich

Description

A comfort food classic compromised of beef, fried vegetables and cheese all wrapped in a crunchy bread roll

Ingredients

  • 1 Tiger roll per serving or tiger baguette
  • 200g beef steak (choose leaner options for a healthier alernatives but almost all cuts work)
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 25g Shredded mozarella
  • 25g Shredded cheddar
  • 1 Onion
  • 20g Oil
  • Salt, Pepper, mixed herbs to taste

Method

  1. Dice your onions and slice your bell peppers into short strips
  2. Slice your beef into thin strips against the grain
  3. Add your salt, pepper and seasoning to your beef
  4. Heat up the oil in a cast iron skillet
  5. Add your vegetables to the oil and stir for 1 minute
  6. Add your beef and let it cook until the water has all dried up and your beef is cooked through
  7. Remove your beef and vegetables from the skillet
  8. Slice your roll/bread in half
  9. Grill the inside until a crust develops
  10. Remove bread and add your beef and vegetable mixture to your sandwich
  11. Add your cheese and sauces of choice
  12. Enjoy
